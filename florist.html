About Us

Welcome to Miezi's, where the love for flowers blossoms into artistry. Meet Miezi, the passionate owner and florist behind the enchanting creations.

Rooted in a deep love for flowers cultivated from her mom's garden, Miezi transformed her lifelong passion into a thriving business. In 2015, she opened the doors to her floral wonderland, aiming to share the beauty of nature's gifts with the world.

Miezi's dedication to quality and artistry is evident in every arrangement that leaves her shop. Whether it's a vibrant bouquet or an elegant centerpiece, each creation tells a story of craftsmanship and love for the art of floristry.

Miezi continues to draw inspiration from the natural world, crafting unique and personalized floral experiences for her customers. Her commitment to sustainable practices and using locally sourced blooms reflects not only her love for flowers but also her desire to contribute to a greener, more eco-friendly community.

She invites you to experience the magic of flowers - where every petal, color, and fragrance is carefully curated to bring joy and beauty into your special moments.
